## Image Best Practices

### General Guidelines
- Use high-resolution images (at least 1920px width for 16:9)
- Optimize images for web (compress without losing quality)
- Use PNG for screenshots and diagrams
- Use JPG for photographs
- Ensure all images are copyright-safe

### Screenshot Guidelines
- Use clean, professional screenshots
- Remove sensitive information (API keys, personal data, etc.)
- Ensure good contrast and readability
- Crop to relevant content
- Use consistent styling across screenshots

### Research Image Guidelines
- Only use images you have rights to publish
- Do not use copyrighted journal figures without permission
- Author-created diagrams and schematics are ideal
- Lab photos should not reveal sensitive equipment or proprietary setups
- High-speed flame images should be from your own experiments
